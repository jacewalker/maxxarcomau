2. Brand positioning for Maxxar Car Care
2.1 Who you are
Maxxar Car Care
Mobile car wash, detailing (inside & out) + window tinting
Service area: Melbourne Bayside & nearby (Brighton, Hampton, Sandringham, Beaumaris, etc.)
2.2 Core brand idea
“Your Bayside Mobile Car Care Concierge.”
You’re not just “a guy with a pressure washer.” You’re:
Convenience-first – like mobile dog groomers and home cleaners
Maintenance-focused – weekly or fortnightly cleans for multi-car families
Premium but approachable – closer to Auto Attention’s quality, but with the friendliness and accessibility of Jim’s.
2.3 Brand pillars
Convenience & reliability
“We come to you – home, office, kids’ sports, anytime that suits you.”
Family & lifestyle fit
“Perfect for busy families and professionals in Bayside who want the car always presentable without thinking about it.”
Premium care & protection
High-quality detailing + window tinting = aesthetics + longevity.
Consistency through memberships
Weekly or fortnightly “maintenance clean” plans for multi-car households.
2.4 Brand personality & tone
Calm, organised, trustworthy (like a good property manager or financial planner)
Lifestyle-friendly, not boy-racer
Plain, warm English; no jargon, no shouting.
